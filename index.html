Who We Are

A hive of strategic thinkers

Mi-Hachi is a dynamic boutique consultancy based in Mozambique, committed to empowering local businesses with tailored, expert guidance. We specialise in business strategy development, strategic marketing, commercial due diligence, and deal sourcing.

Our mission is to foster sustainable growth while creating impactful connections between local companies and international investors. We leverage local insights and strategic partnerships to drive economic progress and enhance community well-being.

The name "Hachi" means "bee" in Japanese — embodying our core values of collaboration, efficiency, and adaptability. Like bees in a hive, we work harmoniously to help our clients achieve their objectives.
