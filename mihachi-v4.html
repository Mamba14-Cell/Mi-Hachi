Questions

Frequently asked

Is this really free?+
Yes — 100% free, no strings attached. The full consultation, AI analysis, and follow-up with Bruno are completely free. No credit card required at any point.
How long does it take?+
About 5–10 minutes to answer the questions. The AI generates your recommendations instantly — you'll have your strategy summary before your next coffee.
Will you sell my data?+
Absolutely not. Your answers are used solely to generate your strategy advice. We do not share, sell, or store your data for any other purpose.
Is this a replacement for a consultant?+
No — and we're honest about that. Think of it as a smart starting point that helps you think more clearly. If you need a deeper conversation, Bruno is available at no charge.
What businesses benefit most?+
Small shops, solopreneurs, local service providers, freelancers, and anyone running a 1–20 person business who needs help thinking through a real decision.
What happens after I get recommendations?+
Implement them yourself, share with your team, or reach out to Bruno for a free follow-up conversation to go deeper on any point.
